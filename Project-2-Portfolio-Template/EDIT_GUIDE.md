# Portfolio Template Editing Guide

## Change Profile Photo

Replace:

assets/images/profile.jpg

with your own image.

---

## Change Name

Search:

Your Name

Replace with your name.

---

## Change Job Title

Search:

Your Role

Replace with:

Frontend Developer
UI/UX Designer
Digital Transformation Student

or your preferred title.

---

## Change Projects

Open:

projects.html

Update:

* Project Image
* Project Title
* Description
* GitHub Link
* Live Demo Link

---

## Change Certificates

Open:

certificates.html

Add:

* Certificate Image
* Certificate Title
* Issuing Organization

---

## Change Resume

Replace:

assets/resume/resume.pdf

with your own resume.

---

## Change Contact Information

Open:

contact.html

Update:

* Email
* LinkedIn
* GitHub
* Phone Number

---

## Change Colors

Open:

css/style.css

Modify color variables according to your branding preferences.

---

## Deploy

Recommended Platforms:

* Vercel
* Netlify
* GitHub Pages

No build tools are required.
